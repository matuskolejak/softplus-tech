Digital Fair Portal

Our flagship project that revolutionizes city fair management through comprehensive digitalization.

Digital Transformation

The Digital Fair Portal project was developed to streamline the work of municipal employees by digitizing and automating repetitive tasks, minimizing manual and paper-based processes, and enhancing overall operational efficiency.

Interactive Vendor Registration

Vendors can register online, select their booth location on an interactive map, and upload required documentation all in one place.

QR Code Integration

Unique QR codes for each vendor allow for quick verification by fair staff and easy access to information for visitors.

Administrative Dashboard

Comprehensive analytics and management tools allow administrators to efficiently oversee all aspects of the fair.

Streamlined Workflow

Our solution drastically simplifies the fair management process by implementing a logical workflow that minimizes bureaucracy and maximizes efficiency.

The system automates vendor verification, space allocation, fee calculation, and documentation management - reducing municipal employees' workload and eliminating error-prone manual processes.

Powerful Administrator Dashboard

The administrative panel provides comprehensive insights into fair operations through visualization of key statistical data and analytical overviews.

Key features include financial indicators, space occupancy metrics, analytical reporting with booth distribution details, and historical data comparisons to optimize future planning.
